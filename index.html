About me

Hello! I am a motivated recent graduate from the University of Washington with a strong foundation in Geographic Information Systems (GIS), data management, and programming. Throughout my undergraduate studies, I honed my skills through rigorous coursework and hands-on internships, always striving to deepen my expertise and contribute effectively to every project.

During my time at UW, I gained practical experience through various internships and as a student assistant with UW Recycling. These roles sharpened my abilities in multitasking, project management, and building professional relationships. I successfully managed tasks ranging from developing web GIS maps for local non-profits to overseeing data management initiatives for the university's recycling program.

I am eager to apply my skills in data management and geospatial analysis to create actionable insights and drive positive change. With a background in environmental studies, I approach each project with empathy and creativity, aiming to impact fields such as transportation, environmental sustainability, and community development. I am excited to bring my dedication and innovative thinking to new opportunities where I can make a meaningful difference.
